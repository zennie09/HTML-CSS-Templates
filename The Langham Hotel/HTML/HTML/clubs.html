CLUB

The Langham Club is a private luxurious haven for guests who prefer a discerning level of comfort. Located on the uppermost floor of the hotel, this 4,000 square foot lounge features panoramic views of Chicago. A breakfast buffet, all-day refreshments, afternoon tea, premium bar service and hors d’oeuvres are served daily. Attentive and personalized service provided by dedicated Club Lounge Ambassadors. In addition, our Writer’s Corner – with reading library and two-computer workstations – looks out over the city.

CLUB PRIVILEGES

Enjoy the following amenities as part of the Langham Club experience: breakfast buffet, all-day refreshments, evening cocktails, and canapés; one complimentary fitness class per stay; two-hour use of the Langham Club Boardroom (subject to availability); Writer's Corner with reading library and two computer workstations; personalized service throughout the stay; complimentary pressing of three garments; complimentary internet access in the guestroom, lounge and other public areas of the hotel.

LANGHAM CLUB AMBASSADOR SERVICES

Savor the superior comfort and privacy accorded by The Langham Club, along with the attentiveness of our Club Lounge Ambassadors, famed for their “service with poise”. Our Club Lounge Ambassadors provide you a variety of exclusive services paired with luxury accommodations, such as travel logistics management, luggage repairs, and even ambassador-drawn baths.

STAY HEALTHY AND RELAXED ON YOUR TRAVELS

Langham Club guests will enjoy one complimentary fitness class per stay for each registered adult. Enjoy access to a variety of classes, offered throughout the day with the ability to choose the one that best fits your schedule.

BOARDROOM ACCESS
